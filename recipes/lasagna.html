Lasagna Bolognese

Description

This is a classic Lasagna Bolognese which features layers of a meat sauce, creamy cheese and tender pasta. The combination of ground beef, Italian sausage, adn three types of cheese creates an irresistible flavor that will have everyone coming back for seconds. This recipe is for 8 people.

Ingredients

  • 0.450kg Ground beef
  • 0.225kg Italian sausage
  • 0.450kg Lasagna noodles
  • 0.450kg Ricotta cheese
  • 0.340kg Mozzarella cheese
  • 0.180kg Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 oz each) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt (divided)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, sausage, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, and pepper. Simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
