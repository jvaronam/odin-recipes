Paella Valenciana

Description

This traditional rice dish from Valencia, Spain, made with a variety of meats, vegetables, and saffron. Authentic paella Valenciana usually includes chicken, rabbit, green beans and white beans. This recipe is for 6 people.

Ingredients

  • 0.500KG chicken, cut into pieces
  • 0.500kg rabbit, cut into pieces
  • 0.200kg flat green beans
  • 0.100kg white beans (garrofó)
  • 2 tomatoes, grated
  • 0.400kg rice (short-grain, e.g. bomba)
  • 1.2 liters chicken broth
  • 100ml olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
  • A few strands of saffron (or 1/2 teaspoon turmeric)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a wide paella pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Brown the chicken and rabbit pieces until golden.
  3. Add green beans and white beans, sauté for a few minutes.
  4. Stir in grated tomatoes and paprika, cooking until reduced.
  5. Add chicken broth, saffron, and salt. Bring to a boil.
  6. Stir in the rice evenly across the pan. Do not stir after this point.
  7. Cook over medium heat for about 15-18 minutes until rice is tender and liquid absorbed.
  8. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
